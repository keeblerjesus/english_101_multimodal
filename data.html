Perhaps the most notorious quote from President Trump was during the Philadelphia Candidate Debate wherein Trump alleges that immigrants were problematic in Springfield, OH. Naturally, nothing was ever substantiated in the affirmative. Notably, all that had occurred is that some random person had made an image with the allegations without citation and it exploded through Right Wing social media and then through Right Wing journalism, and finally it was popularized by is VP pick, JD Vance. All the evidence is to the contrary, including the mayor of Springfield providing a heartfelt account on how positive an effect the Haitian community has been on his town.
